ABOUT

CHRISTINE’S ATELIER is a Millinery, Costume and Product Design brand in the Fashion, Arts and Lifestyle industry which caters to the Male and Female gender irrespective of age and space.

We pride ourselves in excellent and quality delivery of products and services giving clients a flawless appearance with each personalized piece that leaves you looking timelessly elegant and graceful in absolute grandeur.

Formally established in January, 2016, Christine’s Atelier (Formerly, Head Jewels) has in its years of existence been proud supporters of sustainable fashion and lifestyle and ensure that most of our products if not all.

At Christine’s Atelier, Royalty and Sophistication is what defines our handmade pieces made from scratch by manipulating numerous environmentally friendly and repurposed materials as inspired by nature and everyday life. Some of these materials are, soft fabric such as straws, sinamay, wool felt, lace, satin, chiffon and our very own indigenous Nigerian fabrics – damask, aso – oke, Ankara and the hard supplies like plastic, iron, ceramic, etc. carefully crafted with attention to detail using client’s specification and tailor made to suit the desired need as well as giving the client a sense of style, creativity and elegance.

Services

Kids play & learn: We deliver on teaching children the required crafts that will set them apart from their peers and take them to a new level of understanding in handcrafting and ensure they have fun while at it.

After Sales services: If it’s broken, torn or worn, we can fix it and make it look as good as new

Style Consultation: Helping our clients combine an outstanding ensemble from head to toe

Hat In A Box/DIY: Guiding our clients to take a chance and let their creative juices flow by assembling our almost ready made pieces with the help of a manual Bespoke/Exclusive/Tailor made orders according to client’s specification

Rent A Hat: If you just need it for temporary use, we can loan it to you at a fee

Fabric Dyeing: If your outfit is faded and dye-able or you just want to change its color, we turn it around as desired

Skills Training & Empowerment: As part of our community development and empowerment initiative, we organize trainings for the youths in our community and beyond to give them a sense of belonging in the society that will help guide their path with the hope that they will one day establish their own venture as well as paying it forward to others in their various communities. We also train people with plans to start a business of their own someday at a fee
